Future Perspectives

Technology has become so pervasive that one could be easily forgiven for taking it for granted. Enriching and helping the lives of many across the world, it serves as one of the key driving forces behind the continuous (re)evolution of how we interact with each other and the environment around us and serves as a platform for advancing goals which were previously thought of as "impossible". Wireless communications is an example of such a technological advancement, which has undergone many years of evolutionary breakthroughs to bring broadband connectivity to the masses.
As a practitioner in the area of next generation communication systems research , part of my responsibility is understanding and exploring the innovative requirements and techniques that will contribute to the development of existing 4G Advanced networks and the forthcoming generation in mobile communications, i.e. 5G, particularly on the physical layer. Heterogeneous networks, mmWave, Massive MIMO, Internet of (all) Things (device to device communications), are but some of the conceptual and implementable developments that are said to form under the current umbrella of 5G. As you are browsing this page, various stakeholders are in the process of standardising 5G in order to establish a common consensus which can be applied industry wide across networks, protocols and devices. The future of communications is filled with unlimited possibilities that interconnect every part of society, paved, by in part, researchers such as myself, which begs the question: On which (radio) wave will you be riding, towards a more data driven future?
